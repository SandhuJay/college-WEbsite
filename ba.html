Bachelor of Arts :

  • Bachelor of Arts or BA is an undergraduate program in arts.
  • The course can be pursued right after passing class 12th.
  • It is a 3 year course Arts programme.
  • Admission to BA courses are usually based on merit
  • Admission is through university level counselling based on the XII result.
  • AV college offers BA in 8 streams
      1. BA in Telugu
      2. BA in Math
      3. BA in English
      4. BA in Hindi
      5. BA in Public Administration
      6. BA in Political Science
      7. BA in Economics
      8. BA in History
  • Eligibility:
    • Aspirants must have completed their 10+2 from a recognised board.
    • Aspirants should have secured a minimum of 50% aggregate marks at 10+2 level.
