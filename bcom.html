Bachelor of Commerce :

  • Bachelor of Commerce or B.Com is an undergraduate program.
  • Duration: 3 year course
  • Admission is through university level counselling based on the XII result.
  • AV college offers B.Com in 4 streams
      1. B.Com in E-Commerce
      2. B.Com in Finance
      3. B.Com in Economics
      4. B.Com in Banking Management
  • Eligibility:
    • Aspirants must have completed their 10+2 from a recognised board.
    • Candidate must have at least cleared his/her 12th class with minimum 55% of marks in aggregate from Higher Senior secondary. All SC/ST candidate should have 5% of relaxation.
    • Seats:
        • 40
    • Fees:
        • 13k-17k per year(Approx.)
