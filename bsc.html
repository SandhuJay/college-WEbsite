Bachelor of Science :

  • Bachelor of Science or B.Sc is an undergraduate program in arts.
  • It is a 3 year course Arts programme.
  • Admission to BA courses are usually based on merit
  • Admission is through university level counselling based on the XII result.
  • AV college offers B.Sc in 5 streams
      1. B.Sc in Computer Science
      2. B.Sc in Maths
      3. B.Sc in Physics
      4. B.Sc in Chemistry
      5. B.Sc in Biotechnology
  • Eligibility:
    • Aspirants must have completed their 10+2 from a recognised board.
    • Candidate must have at least cleared his/her 12th class with minimum 50% of marks in aggregate from Higher Senior secondary. All SC/ST candidate should have 5% of relaxation.
    • Seats:
      • Computer Science:40
      • Maths:48
      • Physics:48
      • Chemistry:48
      • Biotechnology:36
    • Fees:
        • 15k-20k per year(Approx.)
