AV College of Arts, Science & Commerce Fee Structure

AV College of Arts, Science & Commerce is established under the AV educational Society in Hyderabad City with the support of the Andhra Vidyalaya Education Society. This has been rated by the Magazine “The best Institute” in the city of Hyderabad and Secundrabad. AV college is affiliated with the Osmania University. NAAC has accredited this college as “A” Grade College of Arts, Science, and Commerce. This college offers several graduate and post-graduate programs in the Art, Science, and Commerce departments. The courses are B.A, B.Com, and B.Sc. The AV College admission to these courses will be merit-based directly based on the qualifying examination.

  • B.A- Bachelor of Arts is offered four specializations and the AV College of Arts, Science, and Commerce fee structure. A course will be approximately Rs. 17,095 per annum, and the total will be Rs. 51,285 for 3 years. The intake of the B. A course is 50 seats annually.
  • AV College of Arts, Science, and Commerce fee structure of the B.Com program that must be paid at admission time will be Rs. 22,605, and the annual fee will be Rs. 12,605; hence the complete payment would be Rs. 47,815. The B.Com program will have a yearly intake of 60 seats.
  • The B.Sc. course is offered in 8 streams under the department of Science. The AV College of Arts, Science, and Commerce fee structure will be Rs. 12,605 per annum and specifically during the admission AV College fee for B.Sc. will be Rs. 22,605.

The infrastructure of the AV College, Hyderabad, has a library with an information center. There are centralized facilities for helping students and staff with teaching as well as learning activities. There is no hostel facility at the Av College Arts, Science and Arts. The Auditorium is well designed and has a seating capacity of 500 people at a time.
